# Product

## Register

product

## Users

School administrators managing driver, bus, parent, and student data, along with trip schedule management. They work in a fast-paced school transport environment and need quick access to information, clear status indicators, and guided workflows that minimize errors.

## Product Purpose

BusTrack provides a centralized admin portal for school bus fleet management — tracking buses in real time, managing driver assignments, student rosters, parent contacts, and trip scheduling. Success means administrators can monitor fleet status at a glance, take action on alerts, and keep the school transport system running smoothly with minimal friction.

## Brand Personality

Clean, professional, self-guiding. The interface should feel reliable and trustworthy — a tool that inspires confidence through clarity and consistency.

## Anti-references

- Over-engineered dashboards with dense charts and flashy animations that distract from operational data
- Consumer-like playful UI (bright colors, illustrations, rounded everything) that undermines the professional tone
- Cluttered layouts with competing visual hierarchies
- Dark patterns or hidden navigation that slows down task completion

## Design Principles

- **Clarity over density**: Present information at a glance. Use progressive disclosure for secondary details.
- **Self-guiding workflows**: Every action should be obvious from the interface. Forms, filters, and navigation should anticipate what the admin needs next.
- **Consistent reliability**: Reusable patterns, predictable behavior, and uniform spacing/typography throughout.
- **Status at a glance**: Critical operational states (active, delayed, maintenance, etc.) should be immediately visible through consistent color and icon cues.
- **Error prevention**: Design forms and workflows to prevent mistakes before they happen, with clear recovery paths when they do.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. Sufficient color contrast, keyboard navigable, screen reader friendly, reduced motion support.
